M&T

With Great Clothes Comes Great Styles

M&T is the biggest multinational clothing company. We sold more than 1,000 selection of men's clothing. M&T strive for consistency. We've been selling comfortable and stylish clothes for years. Our stores established in countries on various continents. We frequently work with many notable brands like Gucci, Luis Vuitton, and many more.


Starting as a local Indonesian clothing store in 2002, Budi Wijianto the CEO of M&T, succeeded in establishing the biggest multinational clothing company. M&T started as a formal clothing store. His first store in Jakarta wasn't as big as his rival stores. Since 2004, he started selling clothes for youngsters. Thanks to his breakthrough idea, he became well known all over Indonesia.

In 2010, he opened his first overseas store in Singapore. From that point, he began open his stores in many countries from Japan to USA. As 2019, he is one of the richest man in South East Asia. Until now, his business continue to grow. His brand have been trusted by many other famous brands and customers. His latest store in New York is the evidence of his successful clothing company empire.
